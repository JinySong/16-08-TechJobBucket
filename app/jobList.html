Keep Your Progress

Step 1: Do your research

Dig up enough facts to help you talk intelligently to your potential employer. Interviewers are always impressed when you have conducted your research thoroughly and can ask informed, intelligent questions about the organization and the job.

Step 2: Prep your resume

Analyze the job description. Outline the knowledge, skills and abilities required. Compare your skills and qualifications to the job requirements. Think about what the employer is look for.

Step 3: Submit your Application

Read and follow instructions carefully. Keep your application consistent with your resume. Proofread your application before submitting it. Don't volunteer any negative information. This is a good time to reach out to your professional network.

Step 4: Get ready to interview

Prepare your responses. Go over the job posting and your resume. Dress sharp, look your best, and remember that everything is a learning experience.
